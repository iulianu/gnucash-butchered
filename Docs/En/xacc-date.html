Date Input

The date cell handles the following accelerator keys: 
       '+':
       '=':     increment day 

       '_':
       '-':      decrement day 

       '}':
       ']':      increment month 

       '{':
       '[':      decrment month 

       'M':
       'm':     begining of month 

       'H':
       'h':     end of month 

       'Y':
       'y':     begining of year 

       'R':
       'r':     end of year 

       'T':
       't':     today
GnuCash can be compiled to use either European style dates or US Style dates. Grep for UK_DATES in dates.h
